Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the SSB Psych Tests and our platform.

Are the pictures and words same as real SSB?

Our database contains hundreds of hazy/clear images and psychology words that have either appeared in past SSB interviews or follow the exact thematic patterns defined by the DIPR (Defence Institute of Psychological Research).

Why can't I type my answers anymore?

We updated the platform to be a Notebook Companion Tool. In the real SSB, you do not type; you write rapidly with a pen. We want you to build muscle memory and handwriting speed, so the tool now purely acts as the projector.

Can I pause the timer during the test?

No. To ensure realistic conditioning, the pause functionality is intentionally disabled. In the actual SSB, you cannot ask the assessing officer to pause the projector. You must train under continuous pressure.

PPDT (Picture Perception & Discussion Test) – Day 1

🔹 Process:

  • Show blurred image (30 sec)
  • Write details (1 min)
  • Write story (4 min)
  • Narrate + group discussion

🔹 What is judged:

  • Observation
  • Story clarity (hero, aim, outcome)
  • Communication
  • Confidence + group behavior

🔹 Key Tip:

👉 Simple, realistic, positive story with clear hero

Read PPDT Master Guide

TAT (Thematic Apperception Test)

🔹 Process:

  • 12 pictures (last is blank)
  • 30 sec observe + 4 min story

🔹 Tests:

  • Imagination
  • Problem-solving
  • Personality reflection

🔹 What to include:

  • Hero (your age/gender)
  • Problem
  • Action
  • Logical outcome

🔹 Key Tip:

👉 Show planning + action + success

Read Complete TAT Guide

WAT (Word Association Test)

🔹 Process:

  • 60 words
  • 15 sec each
  • Write first thought sentence

🔹 Tests:

  • Attitude
  • Mindset
  • Values

🔹 Key Tip:

  • 👉 Positive
  • 👉 Short sentence
  • 👉 Action-oriented

❌ Avoid:

Negative, philosophical, and long lines.

Read Complete WAT Guide

SRT (Situation Reaction Test)

🔹 Process:

  • ~60 situations
  • Time: ~30 min

🔹 What to do:

  • Write your reaction quickly

🔹 Tests:

  • Decision-making
  • Practical thinking
  • Responsibility

🔹 Key Tip:

  • 👉 Practical + quick action
  • 👉 Show responsibility & courage
  • 👉 Avoid unrealistic heroism
